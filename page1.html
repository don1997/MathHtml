Repeated Algebra Error

We begin with the expression \[ \sqrt{1-\frac{2-\sqrt{2}}{4}} \] using the rule \[ \frac{a}{b}-\frac{c}{d} = \frac{ad-bc}{bd} \] we get \[ \sqrt{\frac{4-2-\sqrt{2}}{4}} \] At first glance we might just subtract the terms 4-2 and get \[\sqrt{\frac{2-\sqrt{2}}{4}} \] However, \[ \sqrt{\frac{4-2-\sqrt{2}}{4}}\neq\sqrt{\frac{2-\sqrt{2}}{4}} \] Instead we must two basic properties. The Associative and Distributive properties. \[Associative \space property\] \[ (A+B)+C=A+(B+C) \] \[And\] \[Distributive \space property\] \[ A \cdot (B + C) = A \cdot B + A \cdot C \] Utilizing the properties above we get \[ \sqrt{\frac{4-(2-\sqrt{2})}{4}} \rightarrow \sqrt{\frac{4-2+\sqrt{2}}{4}} \rightarrow \sqrt{\frac{2+\sqrt{2}}{4}} \] And thus we arrive at our answer. \[ \therefore \sqrt{1-\frac{2-\sqrt{2}}{4}} = \sqrt{\frac{2+\sqrt{2}}{4}} \]
